Ni WooCommerce Customer Product Report shows the list of customers who purchase that product in product page.

== Description ==

<p>
Product customer report show the list of customer who purchase that product, go to woocommerce edit list any product scroll down the page and find the customer list who purchase that product.
</p>
<p>
To view product customer report by editing the product, product customer report enable the customer list who purchase that product. Customer report provide the option to export the report into CSV, Excel and PDF format. You can sort the report by clicking the header of the report table. 
</p>
<p>
Setting provide the option to control the product customer report columns like billing address, shipping address and customer details.
</p>


<h4> Key feature of product customer report</h4>
<ul>
<li>Dashboard show last 30 Daily sales report, Top 10 product report and Top 10 customer report </li>
<li>Customer report show all billing, shipping and customer details columns, table header sorting</li>
<li>Product customer report export to EXCEL, CSV, PDF, PRINT and copy</li>
<li>Setting option to control the columns of product customer report.</li>
</ul>

2. Activate "Ni WooCommerce Customer Product Report" plugin from admin panel.
3. Admin Menu Name "Product Customer".
